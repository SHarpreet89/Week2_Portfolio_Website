The work primarily involves coding Revit Families and involves use of Python and involves working with Revit API for speedy content management solutions.

Resolution Architecture


Resolution Architecture Sydney appointed me for the design development of a multi-residential apartment building, a stone's throw away from Sydney Opera House. I delivered the Design Development drawing set for Council approval & 3D models depicted in photo above. I ensured the project met necessary requirements.

Alhumaidhi Architects


Alhumaidhi Architect's is a prestigious firm located in the hot middle-eastern country of Kuwait, Working on a dynamic team , I delivered the working drawings of the above photographed weekender villa called "Kit Kat Villa". I was highly commended for my attention to detail and going above and beyond to exceed requirements of the brief.

Tectura Architects


At Tectura Architects, I contributed to the Philip Island Medical Hub project, playing a key role in completing the Design Documentation. I collaborated with the project team using a fundamental AutoCAD-driven platform. Together, we produced the project drawings, leveraging essential skills in teamwork and coordination to ensure successful delivery.

Woods Bagot


Fresh out of university, I was placed onto a $500 million redevelopment project in the heart of Melbourne CBD. As part of a team of six, we successfully delivered the final construction drawings for 80 Collins Street. This role required meticulous attention to detail and effective collaboration to meet the project's high standards and deadlines.

Edmond and Corrigan


At an award-winning Australian practice, I worked directly under AIA Award-winner Maggie Edmond & Peter Corrigan. I handled medium-scale residential projects, ensuring project drawings were updated per engineer and consultant changes. This role expanded my skills in architecture, animation, photography, graphic design, and web design.
